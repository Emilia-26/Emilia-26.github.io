The Path to Pride

The evolution of the modern celebration of a historically suppressed culture may be observed particuarly throughout the 20th century. Spanning, arguably, the most tumultuous historical era, the LGBTQ+ community joined other minority groups, including those leading the Anti-War and Civil Rights movements, in an upheaval of American society, sparking an international cultural revolution, resulting in the designation and recognition of the modern queer community.
